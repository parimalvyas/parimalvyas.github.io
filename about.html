Hello everyone, I am Parimal Vyas. I am a UX/Product Designer currently pursuing a Master's of Science in Human Computer Interaction at Indiana University, Indianapolis.

I have a background in Informatics as my bachelor's degree and have around 4+ of industry experience in the UX domain. It is here that I realized the impact of design in products and how much of a difference is created in the user's day to day lives. This led to know more about how the user's thought and interpretation of information hence I decided to do a master's in HCI which is a fair mix of design methods and psychology.

I aspire to be an emphatic designer who works towards meaningful, informed changes gathered from actionable insights.

I am highly intrigued by slow interaction, tangible interfaces and accessible tech. I enjoy watching TV series, cooking and reading books.
