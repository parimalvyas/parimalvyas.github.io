Context

Why do we feel tired on some days?

During normal sleep, humans cycle through Rapid Eye Movements (REM) and four stages of non-REM (NREM) sleep numerous times a night. When one is repeatedly interrupted and can't cycle normally through these types and stages of sleep, humans may feel tired, fatigued, and have trouble concentrating and paying attention while being awake. [1]

Impacts of sudden waking up

When someone is suddenly forced to wake up they have higher blood pressure and heart rate than those allowed to wake up in their own time. [2]

Sleep quota

Researchers have found that, over the past decade, people have been going to bed later but still getting up at the same time, losing about 40 minutes of sleep on workdays. [3]

The Problems

The Routine-Sleep Cycle is a vicious circle

INSIGHT Disturbance of the sleep cycle should be minimized.

Perceived sleep quota and pre-sleep rituals

INSIGHT Meeting the perceived sleep quota and pre-sleep unwinding.

People perceive alarms differently

INSIGHT Alarms should be less intrusive and more effective.

People have their own reasons for waking up

INSIGHT Waking up fresh so that the goals of the day are met.

Ideation

Smart Watch

The market has a few options such as Apple Watch, FitBit Versa, etc. These have limitations with what they can do due to the form factor the range of sensors that they can hold.

Smart Pillow

Except for ZEEK Smart Pillow, there are not many options. They are not very portable and take up a lot of space.

Smart Eye Mask

Currently, there are sleep masks such as iMore, Lumos, GoToSleep, etc but we want to rethink the space and push boundaries around it.
Holds potential as eye masks are a natural element while sleeping. They are portable and can be used while traveling too.

Solution

WAKE Band

Ergonomically designed product with modern sensors.

    Pressure and Heat-flow Sensors
    Moisture Sensors
    Bio Sensors:
    Brain waves (EEG - electroencephalography),
    Muscle tension (EMG - electromyography)
    Eye movements (EOG - electrooculography)




WAKE App

For Seeping: Select soothing music from the list or a one of your choice.

  • Choose WakeUp Time

  • Track sleep quality

The Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index (PSQI)
It is a self-report questionnaire that assesses sleep quality period of 1-month with 19 Questions and 7 component scores.

âœ…10 out of 19 questions can be answered by sensors embedded in the prototype. Asking the other 9 questions every alternate day is better than asking at the end of the month.

The WakeUp Swipe Game has low cognitive load that presents 5 simple questions with two options each.
These options are designed to measure the mindfulness, alertness, decisiveness, and mood of the user in the morning.


Longitudinal Usability Test (Sleep Quality)


Testing the system with 25 users for 4 weeks of using the Wake Mask and Companion App with data collection by user inputs before sleep, automatic data collection using the sensors during sleep, and WakeUp Swipe game shown above to collect data after waking up.
